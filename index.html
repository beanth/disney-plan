California Adventure

Open to full close

  • Halloween décor is already up. Cars Land becomes "Radiator Screams" after dark.
  • World of Color – ONE runs at night. Worth staying for.

Multi Pass priority

  1. Radiator Springs Racers — book first, longest standby by far.
  2. Guardians of the Galaxy: Mission Breakout
  3. Incredicoaster
Book Racers the second you tap through the gate. Its return window pushes hours out fast.

Dinner — booked

6:20 pm · Lamplight Lounge
  • Book the downstairs location (the reserved, fully themed one). Boardwalk Dining upstairs is a separate walk-up spot.

Final night ever

The two shows end tonight

  • Paint the Night parade — 8:45 & 10:45 pm
  • Wondrous Journeys fireworks — 9:35 pm
Last scheduled night for both (70th anniversary shows stop before Halloween Time). Stake out a spot 60–90 min early.

Fri 8/21

Disneyland · Day 2

Halloween Time opening day
  • Main Street pumpkins go up, and Haunted Mansion Holiday opens with Jack Skellington.
  • Busiest of your three days (Friday + opening day + locals). Lean on Multi Pass hard.

Headliner blitz order

  3. Big Thunder Mountain
  4. Then chain the rest (see churn tip in Info).

  • Frontierland, overlooking the Rivers of America. Vegetarian options on the Southern menu.

Camp by 8:30 pm

Halloween Screams — debut night

  • Runs roughly 9:00–9:30 pm. Confirm the time in the app that morning.
  • Projections hit the castle, Main Street, small world, and the Rivers of America. Castle-adjacent is the spot.
  • Fireworks on select nights, usually weekends. Friday opening night is a good bet.
Opening night + Friday = the heaviest crowd of your trip. Don't count on watching from the restaurant, River Belle closes around 9pm.

Reference & Strategy

🎟Multi Pass, dejargonified
  • Return window = the 1-hour arrival slot the app gives you (e.g. "come 2:40–3:40"). Not whenever you want.
  • The churn rule: you can only hold one booking at a time. Can't book the next until you either use the current one or 2 hours pass.
  • Keep the wheel spinning: between headliners, book quick-loading rides (Pirates, Little Mermaid) whose window is often "right now." Ride it, and it instantly unlocks your next booking.
  • Book at entry: you must be scanned into the park to book. Grab your first pick while walking in.
  • Modify, don't cancel: refresh the "modify" screen to snag earlier return times without losing your slot.
  • One purchase covers both parks at Disneyland Resort.
